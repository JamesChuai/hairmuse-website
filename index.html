Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed directly by Apple. To cancel:

  • Go to your iPhone Settings > Tap your Name (Apple ID).
  • Tap Subscriptions.
  • Select HairMuse and tap Cancel Subscription.

2. Why is my generated photo blurry?

To ensure the best results, please upload a photo with good lighting, facing the camera directly, and without glasses or hats. High-definition results are available for Premium members.

3. How do I delete my data?

We automatically delete your uploaded photos from our servers within 24 hours. You can also manually delete your history in the "Lookbook" tab within the app.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2024

At HairMuse ("we", "our"), your privacy is our top priority. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data, specifically regarding facial data used for AI analysis.

1. Collection of Face Data

To provide our hairstyle try-on and color analysis features, we collect the image you upload. Our AI technology analyzes facial landmarks (such as eyes, nose, mouth outline) to map hairstyles onto your photo accurately.

2. Use of Face Data

The facial data is used solely for the purpose of generating the processed image within the app. We do not use your face data to identify you personally, nor do we use it for authentication or advertising purposes.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

We do not store your face data permanently.

  • Your original photos and generated results are stored temporarily on our secure cloud servers to deliver the service.
  • All data is automatically deleted after 24 hours.
  • You can manually delete your data at any time within the App's "Lookbook" section.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your facial data to outside parties. We utilize trusted cloud providers (e.g., Supabase, AWS) and AI inference partners solely for data processing. These partners adhere to strict data protection standards.
